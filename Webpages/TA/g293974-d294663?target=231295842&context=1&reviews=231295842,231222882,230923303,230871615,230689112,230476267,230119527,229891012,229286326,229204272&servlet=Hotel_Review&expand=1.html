“A Hotel surpassing all our wishes and expectations and more!”
Reviewed 1 week ago NEW

We arrived at the hotel which we booked through their website at about 10 o'clock in the morning and although we knew our room wouldn't be ready, we were checked in straight away and offered an upgrade to one of the top floor suites. We were also treated to a welcome coffee and and biscuits in the lovely inner courtyard. Everybody was very friendly and extremely courteous.
As it was still quite early we decided to go for a walk as it was our first visit in Istanbul. The hotel could not be better located in a small quiet street and perfect for the sites of the "old" Sultanahmet area with literally 5 minutes walk to Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque and Topkapi.
On our return we were shown to our suite or should I call it our apartment. Three terraces gave fantastic views over the Marmara sea and the third one with breathtaking view of Hagia Sophia which looked so near you thought you could touch it.
The suite had a very large sitting and dining area including a small kitchen with coffe machine, fridge etc. A corridor led from the sitting room to the large bedroom with a fantastic four poster bed.
The bathroom had two hand basin areas, a bath tub with jacuzzi and a separate large shower which was also a hamam/steam room all in white and green marble. All amenities were Bulgari and always plentiful. The hotel is relatively small with about 65 rooms and beautifully decorated with local elegant touches and you never feel crowded. The inner courtyard is lovely and tranquil.
The breakfast which is served there is plentiful with excellent choice of Turkish, continental and American choices. I need to touch on the hotel staff as they are really amazingly professional and also warm and welcoming something that really made this trip memorable for us. We would especially want to thank Mr Ipekli who was especially nice to us and to say how much we enjoyed our little chats and his kindness. You should also try and visit the roof top bar with the best views in town of Hagia Sophia.
Whatever else I say about this hotel would not do it justice but if you want to receive true Turkish hospitality at the best hotel in Istanbul then you can't find anything better.
It contributed in us having Relly memorable trip and to want to come back.
A seven star experience in my opinion.

Room Tip: If you can get a room with views of Hagia Sophia. It's a moving experience.
