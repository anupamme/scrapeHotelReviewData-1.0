Friendly boutique hotel in the up-and-coming West of Zürich. Only a few tram stops from the main train station. Plenty of bars and good restaurants nearby. Beautifully thought out and entertaining decor and comfortable rooms. Staff friendly and helpful. Excellent hot and cold breakfast choice, though wouldn't recommend the porridge. Would definitely stay again for business or pleasure.

25hours Hotel Zürich West is the most colourful hotel in the city on the Limmat. Located in the vibrant district 5, designer Alfredo Häberli‘s team has created a relaxed living environment with playful references to the secrets and sights of the city.
