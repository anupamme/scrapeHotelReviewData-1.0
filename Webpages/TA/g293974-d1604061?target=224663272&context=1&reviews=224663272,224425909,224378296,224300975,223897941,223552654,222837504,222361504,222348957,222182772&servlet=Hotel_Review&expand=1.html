“Near perfect in so many ways!”
Reviewed 5 days ago NEW

I stayed here as a solo female traveler and this will be my “go to” property whenever I am in Istanbul. Right after I made my reservation the GM reached out to me to let me know that this is a smaller property, not a big chain type hotel with all the amenities. I appreciated that he reached out just to confirm that I had the correct expectation prior to arrival. I used the hotel transfer service which I would recommend. They charge a flat rate and after a long international flight it was nice to walk out of customs and have someone waiting for me. All you have to do is email the hotel with your flight information and they will take care of it. The charge is added to your hotel bill. The rooms are very comfortable and the bathroom are some of the larger ones, for boutique type hotels, that I have had. I had a king bed and the linen was very relaxing and comfortable. For me the biggest necessity for a good night sleep is a great A/C and it was very hot when I was there. This hotel A/C system is the best! You can’t beat the location. It truly is where you want to be for sites and great restaurants. The only thing I would recommend to the hotel, is they need to refreshed their breakfast buffet. I got there a bit late and most things were gone and not replenished. The view from the rooftop while eating breakfast we beautiful…You will not go wrong booking your stay here!
