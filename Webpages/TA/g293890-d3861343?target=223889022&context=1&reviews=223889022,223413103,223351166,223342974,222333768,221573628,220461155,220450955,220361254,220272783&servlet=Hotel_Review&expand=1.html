Reviewed 5 days ago NEW

Stayed at hotel friends home on two different occasions and both times we recieved amazing service. The staff are wonderful and the hotel is kept super clean. Rooms are comfrotable and clean especially for Nepali standards.
Location: Easy to get in and out of, close to kingsway and not in the heart of thamel which is great! There is construction going on near the hotel so streets are a little more dirty then most but it is minor. The sound from construction is sometimes loud but never interrupts your sleep at night. There are really good restraunts close by such as or2k and a minimart. If you want to be in or near thamel this is the place to be!

I have stayed at many hotels in Nepal over the past three months (4 in Thamel and 1 in boudha) and HFH has been by far the best value with the most friendly and helpful staff. They also have a good breakfast and give you complimentary beverages.

The staff will go above and beyond and are always interested in what you have planned for the day! They want to help you make the most of your time in nepal not just make a profit off of you which is very refrshing especially being a tourist in Thamel! I would take advantage of there travel desk; based on what I have seen and heard from other guests they provide great services.

The hotel also allowed us to leave some baggage for about a month at no additional charge. This was very handy!

“This Hotel Deserves it's Name!”
Reviewed 1 week ago

This hotel is excellent!

I have been in Nepal for nearly one month now, first arriving in Kathmandu and then leaving after two days to travel the country. I returned to Kathmandu today. It is raining heavily, and I had my taxi take me to directly back to Hotel Friends Home.

It has been weeks since I last stayed here, and upon walking in the staff immediately recognized me and warmly welcomed me in. The staff here is so well-trained and helpful. Sandeep, at the front desk, let me know that the hotel was all booked (no surprise, it's excellent), but since it was raining he still offered me complementary tea and coffee, a place to relax and told me that if there is a cancellation he would get me the room or help arrange for my accommodations at another hotel. So nice!! And to my luck, there was a cancellation shortly thereafter.

I am in my room now and writing this review on the fast wifi... and I'm finally in a big, comfortable and clean bed! I haven't been on a comfortable bed since leaving this hotel nearly a month ago (for those who have been to Nepal before, you know luxury is hard to come by). And I am so happy to be back!!

I should also note, importantly, that the owner of this hotel helped arrange my entire month of travel in Nepal. He went above and beyond the services of any ordinary hotel, but that seems to be the way Hotel Friends Home operates - they REALLY do treat you like a friend.

I am so thankful for how Hotel Friends Home helped make a month of traveling in Nepal so easy and enjoyable.

HIGHLY RECOMMENDED!!!
