“Amazing but dated hotel, uneven service”
Reviewed 1 week ago via mobile

For sure the hotel is amazing, the place is beutiful but bare in mind that it is the only one in the neighborhood, the surroundings are not luxury at all.
We stayed in a junior suite, room was nice with dated decoration but it's not shocking. Turndown service with bottled water by the evening and truly accomodating staff (we had a baby they handed us bottles by 5 or 6).
The pool is beautiful but it is not heated and most of the time it is in the shade so water is cool. It's a nice place to hang around though, the lifeguard on duty is helpful with everything.
Food is good, not amazing but service at the restaurant can prove chaotic with kids waiting up to 45 minutes for a plate of spaghetti bolognese.
For the price I would strongly recommend going to hotels on Ipanema beach instead.
