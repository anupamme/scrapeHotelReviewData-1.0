We stayed at the Omni in July 2011, and there were no doubt where to stay on our return to San Francisco this August!

“ACS Conference Stay”
Reviewed 4 days ago NEW

Given that I was on a business trip for the 248th ACS National Meeting, I was therefore given a few option as to where I could stay. I chose the OMNI San Francisco since I had previously stayed at an OMNI while on business in Dallas. The San Francisco hotel was very clean and the decor was tasteful and excellent. Furthermore, if a person wanted to see the sights of San Francisco, this is the perfect location. It is centrally located to common tourist areas such as Fisherman's Wharf, Chinatown, and Union Square, but it is not close enough such that there is heavy tourist traffic in the near vicinity to the hotel, hence the stay is very quiet. Bob's restaurant provides an excellent breakfast selection, however I was not able to enjoy a dinner there, due to other activities. I would highly recommend this hotel to anyone who needed a place to stay for business or for pleasure.
