“Wunderbar”
Reviewed 1 week ago

Even though this hotel situated in district 5, it can be easily reached by tram (tram station right outside hotel) and it only takes 10-15 mins to go to the city centre/main train station.
Staff are super friendly and speak fluent English.
Rooms are clean, with special design. Comparing with other hotels in Zurich (the 'Swiss standard'), and for what you get (the design, the friendly staff, easy tram access), I do think price is very reasonable. However, for wifi signals, it really depends which room you get. Some rooms have better signal while other's signal are very crappy (we had 2 rooms and 1 had better signal) so if you're here for business and wifi is a must, ask for a room with better connection.
If you're a budget traveller but with higher expectation and standard about hotels, this is definitely what you're looking for!
