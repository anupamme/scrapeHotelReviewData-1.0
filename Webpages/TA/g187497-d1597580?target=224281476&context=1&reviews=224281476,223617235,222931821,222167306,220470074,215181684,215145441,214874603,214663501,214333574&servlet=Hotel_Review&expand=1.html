“Away from all the hustle and bustle”
Reviewed 4 days ago NEW

Stayed August 2014. The hotel is located very close to the beach a short walk away and there is also a mcdonalds very closeby. The metro is very close to the hotel as is the bus turistic bus stop which is very convenient. The hotel offers a discount if you buy the tickets for the bus turistic with them. We originally booked a standard room but upgraded to superior for 20 euros per night. It was well worth it as the standard rooms did not come with a fridge whereas the superior one did. Rooms were cleaned everyday although I would not say this is a 4* hotel as the lamp shades were a little dirty and one of the walls had something stuck to it. Bathroom is a decent size and always stocked up everyday with the usual bathroom items. we had a lovely seaview room although two twin beds put together instead of a double one but this didnt bother us. There is a cemetery behind the hotel but it is very well kept and not creepy at all. Staff were very helpful and kind and were happy to answer our questions and also happy to print our boarding passes.

overall a nice stay but not a 4* hotel.

Room Tip: Ask for a seaview room and also upgrade to superior it is well worth it!

My wife and I and two teenage daughters stayed at Hotel 4 for a short-break in Barcelona. The hotel is a short walk to the beach and within easy reach of the city centre by bus, metro or taxi. Close by is Rambla del Pobleneu which has quite a local feel and free of any hassle from beggars or other low life - great for an evening meal and much better value than the city centre. We ate at the hotel one night and the food was very good. We arrived as the restaurant was closing, but the helpful staff were welcoming and laid up a table with no fuss and served up a very good meal.
