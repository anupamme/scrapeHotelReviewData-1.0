“Excellent location with outstanding service”
Reviewed 6 days ago NEW

We stayed in the Neorion Hotel this past August. When we left the airport a driver with our name on a sign waiting who escorted us to the transport provided by the hotel. It was a very comfortable tourist van with reclined seats and lots of legroom. The hotel staff was waiting for us when we arrived and promptly took our bags and offered us a welcome drink. A member of the staff then sat down with us and showed us a map of the city in order to orient us to where we were, the easiest way to get to nearby attractions, and the easiest way to use the public transit systems. They also told us when various sites were open and offered some suggestions for walking tours.

We stayed in a superior room (room 608). While the room was comfortable, I would not book it for the view- we saw the Bosporus only through an array of other buildings, however being up on the 6th floor was great for getting away from the noise and bustle of the city and the balcony was spacious. The room was also fairly narrow, but keeping in mind we were in a large city, I would say that it was fairly spacious.

One of the best things about this hotel is how incredibly accommodating and friendly the staff are! While in Turkey we left Istanbul for two nights to visit Cappadocia and returned for just one night so we could sleep before our flight home. During our excursion the hotel held a portion of our luggage so that we could travel lightly. It was waiting for us safe and sound when we returned. I would definitely recommend this hotel to friends who plan to travel to Istanbul- be it for business or for pleasure.

“One of the best hotels.”
Reviewed 1 week ago

It's almost impossible now to find an original way of praising this hotel. So will simply say that the Neorion is one of the best hotels we've ever stayed in. Anywhere. This is not because of opulent surroundings or luxurious rooms or gourmet food or stunning views from our window. Sure, the hotel is small but quite elegantly furnished. Our room (which had no view) was spacious, clean and comfortable with an excellent bathroom. The food was very good. Absolutely no complaints about anything.
But what distinguishes the Neorion is the warm and friendly atmosphere created by the staff, who go to extraordinary lengths to provide personal attention and service to guests. Nothing is impersonal, from check-in - which is more a welcoming conversation than a transaction over a
counter - to the obliging responses to requests for information, advice, assistance. Everyone smiles. Nothing is too much trouble.
And then there are all the extras that make the hotel so special. The large roof terrace with stunning views across the Bosphorus - accompanied, during 'sunset drinks', by a complimentary glass of wine. The substantial buffet of mezes set out every afternoon for guests to help themselves. The free transfer to or from the airport. The farewell gift of a lovely souvenir scarf.
Thank you Neorion family; we certainly hope to be with you again.
