“Great location, nice hotel!”
Reviewed 2 weeks ago

This Sokos worked out perfectly for us during our final days in Helsinki. It is very conveniently located near the historic Railway Station, Kiasma, and the Atteneum Museum, where there is currently a special exhibition of the art of Tove Jansson, a very famous writer in Finland. Shops, restaurants, Alco, and even a cider pub (I had a microbrew lingonberry cider there) are right around the corner. The breakfast buffet was generous and varied, with large picture windows featuring a view of the Museum of Natural History across the street. Staff at the front desk were very helpful and friendly, an English daily newspaper was provided free of charge, and we enjoyed a restaurant featuring tapas just up a small flight of stairs from the lobby. We also found an excellent tapas restaurant just two blocks away, across from the Railway Station. Convenient, clean, tidy, and a good breakfast--we were very pleased! There is also parking in the garage below the hotel, which is very pricey by U.S. standards, but this is downtown Helsinki, so that it is to be expected.

“A good hotel near the heart of Helsinki”
Reviewed 10 July 2014

We found the hotel to be most comfortable and the staff friendly. It is quite a busy place especially because it has many tour groups. With an all-day transit pass, the hotel is convenient to everything in Helsinki. There are four different tram lines that pass within a couple of blocks of the hotel. Furthermore, Helsinki is an easy walking city. In addition, there are many restaurants nearby.

Now the for the issues. First, breakfast was OK, but nothing like we had in hotels in Oslo, Stockholm, or Copenhagen. Second, as we have been finding, controlling the room temperature is very difficult. While this was the coolest of four that we stayed in, we were unable to cool it down enough for comfortable sleeping. Third, while we had Wifi, access to the Internet was sporadic. Often we would have a connection and it would disappear. A number of people in our group made comments about its lack of reliability, especially when one was halfway through trying to send an e-mail. Finally, the shower (room 809) leaked at the bottom and made the bath mat useless. Simple caulking would solve the problem.

Room Tip: The do not appear to be any bad rooms
