“Great Central Hotel”
Reviewed yesterday NEW

From the word "go" the staff were all really friendly and helpful. The room was spacious, clean, comfortable and well-facilitated, and those beds were incredible! The breakfast, both buffet and a la carte, was varied and of very high quality. The Lava restaurant (where breakfast is served) was also great for dinner, with really technical, well thought out and delicious food. The pool, jacuzzi and sauna/steam room area was really nice and relaxing, while the gym, although limited, was perfectly sufficient and comfortable. The location of the hotel is great, both in terms of being in a nice area and also in terms of being able to walk nearly everywhere we needed to go. A brilliant stay!

“Good Location and Great Interior”
Reviewed 2 days ago NEW

The hotel is stone-throw away from the harbour. Just 5 minutes walk to the waterfront restaurants with a nice selection.

Interior of the hotel is very nice. Luxury yet cosy which I really like. Access to guest floors using the lift require swiping of hotel card, which was not clearly instructed. I have encountered, a couple of times, guests fumbling with the lift buttons when I entered the lift. There were also no staff nearby to assist. Front-desk services were mediocre, efficient nonetheless.

Room was well-appointed. Limited desk space though. Bathroom was amazing and has excellent amenities. Small selection of in-room dining but the food was good.
