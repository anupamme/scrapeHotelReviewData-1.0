“Best Five Star Experience Ever!”
Reviewed 5 days ago NEW

From the moment we arrived, I knew we'd "arrived." We were very early, but they changed our original room to one that would be ready much sooner and offered us tea and biscuits in the lovely front parlor. Our room was unusual with bathroom on one level and bedroom a few steps up, but lovely bedding and decor. Loved the different pillow treats each turndown.

Our breakfasts were delicious served in a charming dining room with a fireplace (no need for extra warmth in July though). The bar was so British library. I'm sorry I didn't have time for a drink there.

Our favorite doorman, Stephen, made us feel safe while we watched a 25K person protest on our street. Very interesting to see something like that for us small town Texas women.

If I ever get back to London I hope I can stay again at the Milestone. It is FIRST CLASS!!!
