“Clean and modern”

Stayed for 3 nights on a business trip in August. Typically I stay at the Fairmont in Calgary but decided to give Le Germain a try. Overall the hotel is very modern and very clean. The bed in my room was extremely comfortable and the linens were very high quality. The bathroom was very large and modern with great toiletries.

The free breakfast that was included was ok, but would have been nice to have a few more options (example: there were no muffins which I thought was odd). The breakfast lounge had plenty of seating though, which was good.

The only aspect of the hotel I did not really care for was the overall "dark" ambience of the hotel. Hallways are very dark, my room was very dark with dark painted ceiling and it felt very "industrial" rather than cozy.
