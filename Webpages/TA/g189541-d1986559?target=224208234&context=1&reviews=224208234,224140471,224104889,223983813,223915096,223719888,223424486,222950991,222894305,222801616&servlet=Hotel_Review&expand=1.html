Reviewed 1 week ago NEW

What a beautiful hotel! We had an excellent 2 night stay here and we didn't want to leave. The iconic building was easy to find which was great as we were driving. The young lady on the reception desk was very welcoming and gave us a room facing the city on the 19th floor. The room itself was a good size, very modern, clean and had a comfortable bed. The bathroom was excellent, but beware of the glass wall if you are sharing with anyone but your spouse! Breakfast was very good, with a wide selection of food: bacon, eggs, breads, cheeses, fruit etc. I did think the car park could have been free (80DK per night) as there was ample parking and not many cars and there is a free shuttle bus from the airport. I would definitely recommend this hotel.
