“Wonderful stay”
Reviewed yesterday NEW

From the moment we arrived for our four night stay, we were treated like VIPs. The hotel is spotless and our room was delightful with a view of the Blue Mosque (sixth floor with balcony). The buffet breakfast is excellent with a wide variety of hot dishes and cold meats and cereals, and during the afternoon a selection of appetizers are available free of charge. There is no bar, but with a rooftop terrace with a wonderful view, who needs a bar? You just ring down and your drinks order is brought to you, and during the sunset hour, drinks are complimentary, a nice touch. The staff are what really makes the hotel special though, nothing is too much trouble and when we needed to get a train, rather than explain the ticket procedure, a member of staff took us to the station and showed us how to work the ticket machine. Flowers on the bed each night, and a lovely gift when we left - as mentioned by so many reviewers, this is really a special hotel and I cannot fault it. Good central location with efficient, helpful and friendly staff.

“Perfect”
Reviewed yesterday NEW

Neorion was terrific value for my one day and night visit to Istanbul. The hotel is tucked into a side street and is perfectly located for exploring the old part of the city in Fatih - minutes to Topkapi Palace, the Hagia Sophia the Blue Mosque and the Grand Bazaar, and an easy to walk over the Galata Bridge to Galata and up to Taksim Square. Complimentary mezzes are laid on in the buffet area if you're around in the afternoon, and a free drink on the roof terrace at sunset - which has a magnificent view of the city; plus there's a pool and Turkish bath in the basement; great after all that walking. The staff are professional and friendly. Neorion has all the charm of a small-scale hotel with none of the downsides. I can't imagine staying anywhere else when I come back here.
