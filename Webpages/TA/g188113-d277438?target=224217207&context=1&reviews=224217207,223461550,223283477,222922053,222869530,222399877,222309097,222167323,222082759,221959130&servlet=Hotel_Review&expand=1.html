“Nice hotel for last night before flying to USA”
Reviewed 6 days ago NEW

This was the last hotel stop of our 10 day vacation before flying back to the USA. It was recommended to us by a friend back home who told us it was "right by the train station". And it IS but I didn't realize there are several train stations in Zurich and I had assumed it was by the main train station. It is actually one stop away at Oerlikon. Not a big deal, but wish I had known that ahead of time. When we got to street level at the Oerlikon station there was a lot of construction and it was confusing to figure out how to get over to the hotel, but once we got there we walked into a large, sleek, contemporary lobby, with lots of activity. Check-in was swift and professional and within minutes we were on our way to the 20th floor. You need your room key to activate the elevators (nice safety touch). The rooms we had were very nice, contemporary design with large windows and great views. Everything we needed was there: fast internet, plenty of outlets for charging our phones and ipad, coffee maker, nice toiletry products in the bathroom, plenty of towels, and interesting reading material on Zurich, to name a few. The air conditioning felt heavenly after spending time in other hotels and guest houses that did not have it. The beds were extremely comfortable (best of the trip) and we loved the pillows. Did not get to the pool, but heard it was great. Did have a drink in the bar downstairs (obviously a popular spot) and a very friendly employee helped us with suggestions of where to go that night. We took the train back into the city center to do some sightseeing which was simple enough, one short train stop away to the main station. Sleeping that night was a pleasure--no noise. We weren't going to eat breakfast there because it seemed pricey, but we ran out of time in the morning so decided to eat there afterall. Good move, because it was fabulous. Wide variety of all kinds of breakfast food, all very fresh and appetizing. The restaurant setting was comfortable and the staff were friendly and funny. Wish we could have lingered longer. It was an easy, short train trip to the airport. All in all, the location is very good for getting around due to its location next to the Oerlikon station. We would definitely stay there again.

Room Tip: We were on the 20th floor. Nice views. Quiet.
