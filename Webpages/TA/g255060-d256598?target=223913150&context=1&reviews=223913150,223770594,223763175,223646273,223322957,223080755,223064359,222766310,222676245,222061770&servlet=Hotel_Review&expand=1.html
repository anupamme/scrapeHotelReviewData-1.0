“Clean and comfortable”
Reviewed 4 days ago NEW

The Mercure is very close to the Sydney airport, which was nice. It is also nearby to many restaurants and shops, as well as the train if you want to go to Darling Harbour or Sydney Harbour. Check in was quick and easy. Staff was very nice and helpful, including front desk staff and concierge. Rooms and bathrooms were nice but small (comparable to any hotel in a large city, like London or NYC.) The bathroom lighting was great, as well as the toiletries. The room was very clean and maintained nicely by housekeeping. We went to the Four Elements restaurant for dinner one night, and although service was slow (I overheard the staff saying how backed up they were due to the rain and an influx of room service orders), the food was delicious! I had the gnocchi and my husband had the lamb rump, and both were excellent! The hotel has a gym and indoor pool, too, but we didn't have time to use it.

The only reason that I gave this four stars instead of five is because of the wi-fi. The website advertises "wifi in your room" but fails to tell you that you have to pay for it. It is hard to believe that in this day and age wifi is not included. They do have complimentary 30-minute access per day, but you have to go down to the front desk to get a login card. There were multiple times that my husband and I went down (from the 7th floor), just to get back up to the room to find that the login we were given did not work, so we had to go back down to get another one. This was extremely frustrating.

Room Tip: We had a city view room, which was nice!
