“Perfect”
Reviewed yesterday NEW

Neorion was terrific value for my one day and night visit to Istanbul. The hotel is tucked into a side street and is perfectly located for exploring the old part of the city in Fatih - minutes to Topkapi Palace, the Hagia Sophia the Blue Mosque and the Grand Bazaar, and an easy to walk over the Galata Bridge to Galata and up to Taksim Square. Complimentary mezzes are laid on in the buffet area if you're around in the afternoon, and a free drink on the roof terrace at sunset - which has a magnificent view of the city; plus there's a pool and Turkish bath in the basement; great after all that walking. The staff are professional and friendly. Neorion has all the charm of a small-scale hotel with none of the downsides. I can't imagine staying anywhere else when I come back here.
