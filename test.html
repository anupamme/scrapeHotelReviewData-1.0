“Great location -- the hill isn't too high!”
Reviewed yesterday NEW

The hills in San Francisco are an attraction on their own. Luckily, to get to this hotel from Union Square you only have to walk two blocks and the hill isn't too steep. If you're heading down to the Financial District or China Town it's also not too bad. There is a cable car stop just outside the hotel but each time we tried to get on it was full; however, a cab ride was only $10 to Fisherman's Wharf, which actually makes it cheaper if there's more than one of you. We had breakfast in the hotel one day and it was delicious--lots of fresh fruit. The concierge was very helpful and recommended a great restaurant strip just down the street. The room was quiet and had plenty of room for luggage and our new purchases. I looked at many hotels before booking this one and I definitely think it was the right choice.

“Comfortable boutique hotel between Union Square and Nob Hill”
Reviewed 1 week ago

This hotel has excellent customer service and is exceptionally clean for the Union Square part of San Francisco. Like most hotels in the area, the room prices are higher (1.5 - 2.0 times regular price) during busy times and conventions, so try to book for times of low demand. The rooms are spacious and clean, the beds are comfortable, and the HVAC is very quiet. In addition to a mini-fridge (the exorbitantly priced mini-bar), our room had an in-room air purifier! The front desk and concierge staff were always polite and helpful. Because the hotel is "up" near Nob Hill, the rooms tend to be quieter than those in hotels directly around Union Square, but Powell Street near the hotel is very steep (emphasis on both very and steep). The hotel restaurant has limited service (usually just breakfast and cocktail hour); not to worry -- this area has plenty of restaurants for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The bed turn-down service in the evening includes a square of yummy chocolate for each occupant! When in San Francisco, we always try to book this hotel.

“Pleasant Stay at The Orchard Hotel”
Reviewed 1 week ago

I recently enjoyed a pleasant stay at The Orchard Hotel in San Francisco. Had a lovely time in the city and walked all over the city. The hotel is located opposite the famous cable car line, just a few minutes walk downhill to one end of the Powell line which can take you to Fisherman's Wharf. Be warned, the queue can be very long and you may have a long wait. The area around the hotel is Union Square with its shopping malls. There are plenty of bar and dining opportunities in the area along with Chinatown. Getting to and from the hotel, I recommend the shared van. Was just US$16 per person one way, very reasonable.
Whilst the lobby to the hotel looks slightly dated, the room itself is very clean and has everything you need in a room. The customer service no the reception desk is very helpful. Highly recommend this hotel if staying in San Francisco.
