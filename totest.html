“Great hotel! Fantastic service! Convenient location!”

For our long weekend trip to Istanbul we chose to stay in Neorion hotel in the Old City. Before had checked many hotels and driven by the enormously positive reviews on trip advisor we decided on Neorion. Best choice by far!
Location is very convenient for easy access to all historic icons of the City. It takes literally 10 to 20 minutes to walk to Aya Sofiya or Blue Mosque and Basilica Cistern or The Grand Bazaar.

Hotel itself is pretty modern and decorated very tastefully. It is usually not easy to achieve the right ambience together with comfortability but Neorion managed that beautifully!
The room was very comfortable & pleasant. Bathroom facilities top notch.

The lobby again very comfortable and nicely decorated with replicas of Byzantine artefacts on display. In the background nice music adding to the relaxing feel.
There is a spa in the hotel, where guests can enjoy all the treatments.

Staff and their service alleviates this hotel to a different level. All of them are very professional, speaking flawless English, attentive yet not over keen. Making reservations, whether at a restaurant or hammam was not a problem at all!

Can't praise the hotel enough and have seen quite a few in my travels.

Recommended to all discerning travellers seeking good quality and pleasant accommodation in the great city of Istanbul!

“Very special. A real gem.”
Reviewed 6 days ago NEW

This hotel has all the major things - great location, spotlessly clean, wonderful linens, modern bathrooms with waterfall shower heads - but it is the small things that make it so special. The service is amazing, the food is great, and their local knowledge is spot on. Let's start with the food... Breakfast is served buffet style and includes fruit, breads, cereal, cheeses and cold cuts, scrambled eggs, bacon or sausage, potatoes, etc. Enough to give you a great start to the day. Then there are the "hors d'oeuvres" served from 1:00 to 7:00 PM. Believe me when I say you can make this lunch or an early light dinner. Coupled with the free glass of wine up on the roof top terrace watching the sunset it is sheer magic.

Let's move on to the service. They have a Guest Relations person - thank you, Loretta - who will sit down with you and plan out all your sight seeing needs, how to get around on public transportation, what to see, when to see it. There are so many incredible things to see and do in Istanbul that you will have to pick and choose. The front desk is always ready to help. Need a Turkish wall plug? Change? A taxi? Just ask. Nothing is too much trouble.

Lastly, I must talk about the Hammam, or Turkish baths, in the basement. There is a small swimming pool, a jacuzzi, a sauna, and the traditional steam Turkish bath, all free. The only added cost is for a "salt scrub" and/or a massage. Don't miss out on this. If you only have a short time, have the scrub. You can get a massage anywhere in the world, but the full Turkish bath experience is unique to this area. My skin was polished and I smelled like a rose - literally!

We would return here in a heart beat! Thank you to all.
